Berry Protein Shake

Description

I love this shake so much, I make this shake for myself almost every day. It's quick, easy, and tasty! Plus, it'll make sure you get lots of fruit and protein in your day.

This recipe does require a blender, so if you don't have one unfortunately you won't be able to follow.

Ingredients

  • 300 ml of milk
  • 45 g of protein powder
  • 100 g of frozen berries
  • 5 g of the thing (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the milk, protein powder, berries, and dust in the blender.
  2. Blend!
  3. Now it's ready to serve.
