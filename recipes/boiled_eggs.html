Boiled Eggs Recipe

I like to boil my eggs in the pressure cooker! It's so easy, and doesn't use much water or take too much attention.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs.
  • 1 cup of water.

Steps

  1. Put the water in the pressure cooker, and put the eggs inside on a steaming platform.
  2. Pressure cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Put the eggs in cold water for a minute.
  4. Peel, and ready to serve!
